ABOUT STEAM LABORATORIES

EXPERIMENTAL DEVELOPMENTS

These projects are still in need of improvement. We will develop something, but we will postpone something as unnecessary. We hope that your feedback will help improve a lot of the experiments and make them part of Steam. This is how our labs are set up.

FOR WHAT?

We always try to bring something new to Steam, but in the past, our work was only announced if it was planned to be part of the platform. The Steam Labs allow us to share these ideas and improve them with your feedback before they go live on Steam.

WHAT'S NEXT?

Like other Steam features, the labs are also kind of an experiment. It needs to be improved, which means that your feedback would not hurt us. We hope that you will share your ideas and impressions in the discussions, and look forward to joint experiments.
